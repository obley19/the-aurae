Our Heritage

Bridging Classical Techniques & Contemporary Taste

Born from a passion for both vibrant Asian ingredients and the disciplined art of French baking, The Aurae is a celebration of cultural harmony. Every creation in our kitchen tells a story of embrace and precision.

We use the finest matcha from Uji, yuzu from Kochi, and butter from Normandy. True aesthetic pleasure lies not just in visual perfection, but in the surprising, lingering notes woven into delicate buttery layers.
